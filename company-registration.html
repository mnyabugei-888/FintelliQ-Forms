◆ What We Handle For You — Start to Finish

You provide the details. We handle everything on eCitizen, KRA iTax, NHIF, NSSF, and the Business Registration Service. By the time you leave our office, your application is submitted and you are simply waiting for your Certificate of Incorporation, PIN, and compliance certificates to be issued.

The Registration Journey
01
Business Name Search & Reservation
We search eCitizen / BRS to confirm your preferred name is available. Up to 3 name options checked. Name reserved immediately upon confirmation.
Same day
02
Preparation of Incorporation Documents
We prepare your Memorandum & Articles of Association, CR1 form, statement of nominal capital, and all director/shareholder declarations.
1–2 days
03
eCitizen Submission & Government Fees
Full application submitted via eCitizen BRS portal. Government registration fees paid (separate from our service fee — we advise exact amounts per entity type).
Day 2–3
04
Certificate of Incorporation Issued
Government processing time: 3–7 working days (standard) or 1–2 days (express). We monitor status and notify you immediately upon issuance.
3–7 working days
05
KRA PIN Registration — Company
Company PIN registered on iTax using the Certificate of Incorporation. Required before any tax obligations or bank account opening.
Same day as certificate
06
NHIF & NSSF Employer Registration
Company registered as an employer on NHIF and NSSF portals. Employer numbers issued. Required if you have or plan to have employees.
1–2 days
07
County Business Permit (Single Business Permit)
Application submitted via eCitizen for your county business permit. Required to legally operate from your business premises.
3–5 working days
08
VAT Registration (if applicable)
VAT registration on iTax if your business turnover exceeds or is expected to exceed KES 5M per year, or if you choose voluntary registration.
2–3 days
◆
Post-Registration Advisory Call
30-minute call to walk you through your obligations — when to file, what to pay, how to open a business bank account, and your first steps as a registered company.
